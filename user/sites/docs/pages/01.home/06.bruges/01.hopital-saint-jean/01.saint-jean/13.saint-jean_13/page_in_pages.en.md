<sup>chap.9</sup>  
<sup>01</sup> 
Then the fifth angel blew his trumpet, and I saw a star that had fallen from heaven onto the earth, and the angel was given the key to the shaft leading down to the [color=#e6a64d]abyss[/color].    
<sup>02</sup> 
When he unlocked the shaft of the abyss, smoke rose out of the abyss like the [color=#e6a64d]smoke from a huge furnace[/color] so that the sun and the sky were darkened by the smoke from the abyss,  
<sup>03</sup> 
and out of the smoke dropped [color=#e6a64d]locusts[/color] onto the earth: they were given the powers that scorpions have on the earth.  
<sup>04</sup> 
they were forbidden to harm any fields or crops or trees and told to attack only those people who were without god's seal on their foreheads.  
<sup>05</sup> 
They were not to kill them, but to give them anguish for five months, and the anguish was to be the anguish of a scorpion's sting.  
<sup>06</sup> 
When this happens, people will long for death and not find it anywhere; they will want to die and death will evade them.

<sup>07</sup> 
These [color=#e6a64d]locusts[/color] looked like horses armoured for battle; they had what looked like gold crowns on their heads, and their faces looked human,  
<sup>08</sup> 
and their hair was like women's hair, and teeth like lion's teeth.  
<sup>09</sup> 
They had body-armour like iron breastplates, and the noise of their wings sounded like the racket of chariots with many horses charging.  
<sup>10</sup> 
Their tails were like scorpions' tails, with stings, 
and with their tails they were able to torture people for five months.  
<sup>11</sup> 
As their leader they had their emperor, the angel of the abyss, whose name in hebrew is Abaddôn, and in Greek Apollyôn (which means: Destructor). [color=#999]\(…)[/color]  
<sup>chap.10</sup>  
<sup>01</sup> 
Then I saw another powerful [color=#e6a64d]angel[/color] coming down from heaven, [color=#e6a64d]wrapped in cloud[/color], with [color=#e6a64d]a rainbow[/color] over his head; his face was like [color=#e6a64d]the sun[/color], and his legs were [color=#e6a64d]pillars of fire[/color].    
<sup>02</sup> 
In his hand he had [color=#e6a64d]a small scroll, unrolled[/color]; he put his right foot in the sea and his left foot on the land.

<sup>06</sup>

<sup>chap.12</sup>  
<sup>01</sup> 
Now a great sign appeared in heaven: [color=#e6a64d]a woman[/color], robed with the sun, standing on the moon, and on her head a crown of twelve stars.  
<sup>02</sup> 
She was pregnant, and in labour, crying aloud in the pangs of childbirth.  
<sup>03</sup> 
Then a second sign appeared in the sky: there was [color=#e6a64d]a huge red dragon[/color] with seven heads and ten horns, and each of the seven heads crowned with a coronet.  
<sup>04</sup> 
Its tail swept a third of the stars from the sky and hurled them to the ground, and the dragon stopped in front of the woman as she was at the point of giving birth, so that it could eat the child as soon as it was born.

<sup>05</sup> 
The woman was delivered of a boy, the son who was to rule all the nations with an iron sceptre, and the child was taken straight up to god and to his throne, 
<sup>06</sup> 
while the woman escaped into the desert, where god had prepared a place for her to be looked after for twelve hundred and sixty days.  
<sup>07</sup> 
And now war broke out in heaven, when Michael with his angels attacked the dragon. 
The dragon fought back with his angels, 
<sup>08</sup> 
but they were defeated and driven out of heaven.  
<sup>09</sup> 
The great dragon, the primeval serpent, known as the devil or Satan, who had led all the world astray, was hurled down to the earth and his angels were hurled down with him.
